About Me

Claire Durst, a Maryland native, graduated Drew University in 2015. There she obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Sociology with a minor in Arts Administration, with a focus on nonprofit management. Claire also served on the University admissions department as a tour guide and sang in multiple choirs. After serving a year in AmeriCorps she has decided to take on a new skill, coding.
